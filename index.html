A 2D top-down survival horror game

Haunted Spot

Haunted Spot is an asymmetrical multiplayer top-down pixel-art game where a team faces a playable vampire and an AI-controlled ghost entity

Matches take place inside a haunted manor as the team completes tasks by interacting with objects to gather clues and unlock an escape route while avoiding the vampire

Combat is fast and lethal, with a two-shot kill system, and the ghost influences the pacing to keep tension high
